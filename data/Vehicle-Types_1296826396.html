Open the Vehicle and Vehicles types master data screen using the master data overview (:regeldefinition: symbol in the cadis control panel)

You must define vehicle types before you define vehicles, because you must enter the vehicle type for each vehicle.

Vehicle types define common characteristics of a vehicle that are important for scheduling and route optimization, such as dimensions, weights, suitability for dangerous goods, tail lift, etc.

Vehicle Types

The fields marked with an asterisk are mandatory.

However, it is a good idea to fill in all fields that are important for planning decisions and capacity planning and are checked in dispatching checks, such as

  • Tail lift

  • Long goods

  • Dangerous goods

  • Loading capacity

  • Pallet storing areas

Vehicles

Everything that has wheels in cadis is defined in vehicle master data, including both vehicles that drive themselves as well as trailers and semitrailers. A fixed trailer can be defined for tractor vehicles, which is itself defined in the master data.

The fields marked with an asterisk are mandatory.

Only enter the vehicle type In the Vehicle Type ID block. All other fields are taken from the vehicle type master data and are only displayed here.

The Vehicle availability

You can define your vehicle availability throughout the weekdays. Your settings will be considered by cadis in trip optimization & availability will be displayed clearly during planning.
