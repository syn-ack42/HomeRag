CADIS Knowledge Base : The Master Data

Created by Julian Beyer, last modified on Aug 21, 2025

The Master Data refer to a set of data defined within cadis or imported from your host system, that are not meant to be changed on a daily basis. Those data will be used to manage operations & shipments.

You can also reach the Master data overview screen using the :regeldefinition: symbol in the cadis control panel.

You can update the following master data:

Transport resources

  • Freight Carriers & Drivers : They can be attached to vehicles but you can change the freight carriers manually at departure level which is not the case for drivers. if you choose not to attach vehicles & drivers, the latter can be identified in cadis through their mobile access.

  • Vehicles & Vehicle Types : Create 1st the vehicles types (Ex: Van, trucks…) available in your depot & define their properties (weight & volume capacities, tail lifts, dangerous goods…). Then you can create your vehicles by giving them an ID & a selecting a vehicle type (2 mandatory fields). You can also define the vehicles availability, their belonging to freight carrier partner & a driver if one is permanently assigned

  • Swap bodies / Containers & Container Types : Same principle as for the vehicles, create 1st the types, (Ex : 20′ or 40′ containers, swap bodies…) before creating the trailers.

Organisation & Admin

  • The Routes: Of type Line Haul for transport operations between agencies or Last Mile for deliveries, collections or direct transport (from shippers to consignees)

  • The Handling Instructions : They can apply at any operational stage ((Un)Loading, Delivery, Inventory, General)

  • The Terminal Areas & Locations : storing positions in your agency. Locations are included in Areas. Planned storing positions can be defined from cadis back-office. Actual positions must be scanned in cadisAPP.

  • The Stop locations : They include a StopID that your host system can send for every shipment to cadis. This way, if your consignees or shippers name is written differently, cadis can create a match between Stops & shipments.

  • The Routings : They allow you to define an outbound route for your shipments based on the consignees or shippers postal codes. The Outbound Route refers to the next departure (Last Mile or Line Haul) a shipment or collection is to be dispatched to.

  • The Customers

  • Area freight forwarders

The Delete and Open buttons are run on the selected line. Create creates a new data record. To edit a data record, you can also double-click on it in the overview.

You can export ALL items of a master data from an agency & import them towards another agency of your network. You can also save the master data under a CSV file.

Scope of application

Master data that you create is valid in the depot for which you are registered.

By importing it, master data can also be available to all depots or for specific depots within your organisation. You can see the validity in the Depot column.

You cannot change master data that does not have your depot as depot in the detail screen (Edit screen).
