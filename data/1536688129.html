Master Pallet

The detail mask for a master pallet can be opened via the context menu "Sheet" (to be renamed "Details") or by double-clicking on a master pallet. Access is possible both from the shipment overview and from the master pallet list in the processes Loading, Unloading, and Inventory.

The mask for the master pallet is not editable. All displayed information is for viewing purposes only and cannot be modified or adjusted in this area.

The menu is located at the top of the mask under (⋮) and offers the same functions as the menu in the shipments table. (Shipments )

Tabs

The tabs on the left side represent different sections or categories within the application. Each tab is labeled with a name and, in some cases, a symbol. In brackets next to the tab name, the number of entries or elements available in that respective section is displayed.

Master Pallet

The "Master Pallet" tab provides an overview of all essential information for the selected pallet:

  • Number of shipments: Displays how many shipments are fully or partially assigned to this master pallet.

  • Number of packages: Overview of assigned as well as unassigned packages.

  • Master pallet details: Created by, creation date and time, master pallet barcode, type and status of the master pallet, actual storage location, last scan (date/time), outbound route, target relation, departure, unloading list, EDI status.

Shipments

The "Shipments" tab lists all shipments assigned to the master pallet. Details about each individual shipment are visible and selectable here.

Photos

In the "Photos" tab, all photos linked to the master pallet in any process are displayed as thumbnails. For each photo, supplementary information such as label, date taken, and a related identifier are shown. The images are for viewing purposes only and cannot be edited or deleted in this area.

History

The "History" tab displays all actions and status changes logged for the master pallet in tabular form. For each entry, details such as level, type, source, user, code, description, as well as timestamps for status and entry, are visible. The associated job reference is also included.
