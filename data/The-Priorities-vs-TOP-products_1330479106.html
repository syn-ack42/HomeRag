Principles

cadis allows you to define priority levels for shipments, referring to a numerical scale, not to be confused with the TOP products, which are based on a binary system.

PRIORITIES – The priorities are only calculated once a day because of performance reasons (the calculation time can be configured) & at shipment import to cadis. It can be defined using 3 ways :

  1. by EDI import from your host system (see section 1)

  2. manually, at shipment level (see section 4.1)

  3. by mean of the rules engine (see section 4.3)

TOP PRODUCT – The Top products are define at Master Data level, along with the Product codes. On a daily basis, your host system informs cadis of the shipments product codes, thus inheriting of the attribute TOP product, or not.

The differences in EDIs

Prioritiy levels & Top Products can be communicated to cadis by your host system through EDI files. The tags used are different:

Priorities

TOP products

<item:Priority>0</item:Priority>*

<item:isTopProduct>true</item:isTopProduct>***<item:ProductCode>Imp</item:ProductCode>***

*Higher values mean higher priority. A value range of 0..9 is recommended.

**Create your product codes at Master data level. It is at this stage that you may define the product codes TOP. The product codes should be communicated to cadis teams for configuration.

***Communicate your shipment’s product codes to cadis. The shipments will consequently inherit the attribute TOP product (or not).
