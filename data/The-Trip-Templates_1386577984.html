CADIS Knowledge Base : The Trip Templates

Created by Mohamed.Shebl, last modified by Julian Beyer on Sep 08, 2025

Open the trip templates

After creating your trips in the master data, you can configure them in the trip templates. The daily departures you will generate afterwards will have your configurations saved.

❶ Open the menu “Rules & Templates”

❷ Open the tab “Trip templates”

❸ Select the transport type you wish to work on. Line hauls are transport operations for one agency to another & Last Mile refers to transports for deliveries or collections at customer

❹ The trip templates already created are listed. You can double-click on a row to edit the template

The details of trip configuration

If you set a departure offset (for example +1 day), the trips you will generate at day D will appear for loading at D+1. In that case, the arrival date will be also set to D+1 (same day as departure day).

Generate your daily departures

❶ Click on "Generate"

❷ Choose a range of date during which the daily departures will be generated. Click on "Apply"

❸ Cadis shows you which departures were succesfully generated during the selected range of days

❹ You can find the generated departures in the menu "My Loadings". You can now dispatch shipments to those departures & release them for loading.
