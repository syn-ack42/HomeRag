CADIS Knowledge Base : Reports and Statistics

Created by Julian Beyer, last modified on Aug 19, 2025
  • 1 Frequent selection criteria
  • 2 Print buttons

Open the Reports and Statistics screen using the :berichtStatistik: symbol in the cadis control panel.

In the left area, select the report you want to print.

In the right area you select what is to be printed. It changes depending on the report. For some reports you can choose between different sorting orders.

Frequent selection criteria

  • Vehicle and Date
    Explanations in the screen. If you use vehicle areas, you must specify whether the vehicle numbers are numeric (integer) so that the data range can be evaluated correctly

  • Period from – to

  • Date (a day)

Print buttons

  • Print
    Prints the report directly to your current printer.

  • Print Preview
    Opens the report in the PDF reader. You have the option to print on a printer of your choice or to save it as a PDF.

  • Send as Email
    A new e-mail message is opened in your e-mail program and the report is enclosed as an attachment. You enter the consignee address, subject and other text.
