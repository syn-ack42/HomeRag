CADIS Knowledge Base : Dimensions & Weight

Created by Mohamed.Shebl, last modified by Julian Beyer on Sep 08, 2025

Visualize oversized shipments in cadis back-office or document dimensions & Weight of packages in cadisAPP

Visualize the weight in cadisSTUDIO

In cadisSTUDIO, you will find the weight of your shipments in different screens like in the First & Last mile menu, tab “Planning”, in the “Weight Structure” block. Click on any figure to visualize the shipments matching the weight category. These data are sent to cadis by your host system.

You will also find the weight of your shipments in the trips details

Edit the Dimensions & Weight in cadisAPP

In the ConfigManager

First, you must enable the possibility for cadisAPP users to edit the Dimensions & weight of packages. This action is to be done separately for each process (Un)Loading of Linehauls &/or Local Traffic + Inventory.

Process in cadisAPP

❶ At package level, open the context menu, click on “Edit dimensions & Weight”

❷ Enter the dimensions of your pallet

❸ An icon at shipment & Package level shows that dimensions were changed
