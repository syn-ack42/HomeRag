CADIS Knowledge Base : Drivers Management

Created by Julian Beyer, last modified on Aug 21, 2025

You can create your drivers profiles & assign them to specific trips or you can select them on daily basis in your trip departures

Create your drivers in the Master Data

Open in cadisSTUDIO the menu “MasterData”, then the tab “Drivers”

Click on “Create” for a new driver or double click a row to open detailed data. The fields marked with an asterisk are mandatory. The ID must be unique.

Click Save to record the changes and Cancel to discard them.

Select your drivers for trips

At configuration stage, you may select your default drivers in your vehicle Master data or in your Trips Templates

At vehicle level

In your trip templates

❶ Open the details of a vehicle to edit (for more details, see Vehicles - CADIS Knowledge Base - Confluence)

❷ A dedicated field is available for selecting your driver. The options in the drop-down menu are sourced from your Drivers Master Data, which was set up in part one of this article

❸ Optional: You can include a vehicle in your trip template. This will automatically fill in the vehicle field when generating daily departures, along with the driver linked to that vehicle

❶ You may select your driver directly in your Trip Template where a dedicated field exist. This will automatically fill in the driver field when generating daily departures

❷ The selected driver will show in your trips overview

NOTE: The driver assigned directly in the trip tremplate prevails as default driver to the one assigned at vehicle.

Generate your daily departures (for more details, see The Trip Templates - CADIS Knowledge Base - Confluence)

Export to your host

At closure of the loading lists, cadis exports to your host system the LoadingStatus which include both the default & the real drivers
