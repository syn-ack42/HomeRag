CADIS Knowledge Base : How can I get rid of completed shipments?

Created by Mohamed.Shebl, last modified by Julian Beyer on Sep 08, 2025

Example: A shipment/collection that can still be dispatched has already gone out/ is already collected, outside of cadis.

Shipment: You can disqualify the shipment by context menu entry.

Collection: You can cancel the collection by context menu entry.
