CADIS Knowledge Base : The Handling Instructions

Created by Mohamed.Shebl, last modified by Julian Beyer on Sep 08, 2025

You can define Handling Instructions in cadisSTUDIO & have them displayed on cadisAPP at (Un)Loading, Delivery or simple scan. The instructions can apply at shipment, stop or list level.

Create your Instructions Master Data

❶ Click on « Create »

❷ If the case “Important” is ticked, a dialog screen will pop-up in CADIS APP at package scanning. If “Trip relevant” is ticked, the handling instruction will be displayed prominently on trip level (so the driver can easily see them when reviewing the trip).

❸ Save your work

The Instructions at shipment level

After you have created your handling instructions master data, you can send them from your host system to cadis in the shipments EDI, create them manually, assign them automaticaly from your rules engine or define them in your recurring pick-up orders.

How are Instructions transferred by EDI

❶ In the shipments EDIs sent from your host system should be mentionned the handling instructions code. Their type decides at which logistic stage the instruction will be displayed in cadisAPP. You can also send an additional text.

❷ In cadisSTUDIO, while shipments planning, you can already read the instructions

❸ A dialog will pop-up in cadisAPP during operational processing if the instructions are set as Important

How to manually create an Instruction or define it in the collection template

The instructions defined in the collection templates will apply to all collections created from that template. The process is similar to the manual creation.

The manul creation

In the Collection template

❶ Open the details of a shipment/collection template

❷ In the Instructions tab, click on “Create”

❸ Fill in the 3 mandatory fields as requested

❹ Apply your changes & Save

How to configure your rules engine

The instructions automatically assigned by your rules engine apply during delivery & pick-up only, not intra-agency processes ((Un)Loading, Inventory…)

❶ Open the rules engine dedicated to Handling Instructions

❷ Select from a drop down list your requirement

❸ Enter your criteria (free text)

❹ Select your instructions master data. The drop down list is feeded by your master data creation

❺ You can enter an additional text for your instruction. It’s a free text that is optional

The Instructions at stop level

The instructions can be entered in your stops master data. They will apply to all orders matching with this stop

❶ In the Stop locations master data, select a previously created stop & open it’s details

❷ In the propertires (Delivery or Collection), fill in the requested fields. You can choose the Process in which your instruction should apply

❸ Save your work

The Instructions at list level

In the Loading list details, open the tab “Dispatching Info”. The Planned storing position you will select & Instructions you will enter apply apply to all the shipments scheduled in this loading list.

They will be displayed in cadisAPP at any other stage (unloading, inventory or Infoscan). Their objective should be to notify Terminal Workers that these shipments should be prepared for this specific loading.

When the loading list is closed, the dispatching info is deleted at shipment level

These instructions apply in agency only & have a Max caracters = 500

A pop-up will appear in cadisAPP at 1st scan in a process different from loading

The instruction & the planned storing position remain displayed
