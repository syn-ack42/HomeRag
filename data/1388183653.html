CADIS Knowledge Base : Why can´t I start the stop sequence optimization for my trip?

Created by Mohamed.Shebl, last modified by Julian Beyer on Sep 08, 2025

Check the geocoding of all stops. As soon, as one stop is not geocoded, the stop sequence optimization does not work. After geocoding the stop manually, you can start the stop sequence optimization.
