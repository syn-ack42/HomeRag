CADIS Knowledge Base : Principles & Configuration for the Loading Equipments

Created by Mohamed.Shebl, last modified by Julian Beyer on Sep 08, 2025

Loading Equipments (LDM) refer to packaging types such as pallets, cartons, bags, etc. Cadis allows drivers to document the quantities of these equipments loaded, unloaded or exchanged with customers

You can check our page for detailed guide of the feature in cadisSTUDIO: https://cadissoftware.atlassian.net/wiki/x/IoClUg

Definition & purpose

❶ Your Host System sends to cadis a shipment including a pallet that is exchangeable (this is defined at master data level)

❷ At collection stop, the driver records the pallet exchange with the shipper using cadisAPP. When the stop is closed the pallets quantities are exported to your host system

❸ After a linehaul loading is closed, pallets actual quantities are transmitted with loaded shipments from departure to receiving agency

❹ Same as for collection, at delivery stop, the driver records the pallets exchange with the consignee using cadisAPP. When the stop is closed the pallets, quantities are exported to your host system

Configuration

Terminal Handling

At loading &/or unloading, you can enable the pallets quantities registration by cadisAPP users or compel them to do so. cadis ConfigManager allows you to create Depot Groups so that the config can be different per depot

In the Config Manager (❶), section “Terminal Handling” (❷), select the process of loading or unloading (❸), then Linehaul or Local Traffic (❹).

Choose to tick the boxes depending on your wishes

Note: You can define an Approval barcode that drivers will be compelled to scan if they report a discrepancy on exchangeable pallets

Pick-Up & Delivery

At delivery &/or collection, for each of your delivery statuses, you can make the pallet exchange mandatory or simply enable it. You can also require a signature from your customer to confirm the good exchange

In the Config Manager (❶), section “Transport Execution”(❷), sub-section “select the process of loading or unloading “Mobile Workflow configuration”(❸). Select then delivery or collection workflow you wish to configure (❹).
