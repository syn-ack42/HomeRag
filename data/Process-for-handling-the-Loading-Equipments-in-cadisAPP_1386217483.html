How to visualize the expected pallet types & quantities

The number of pallets visible in cadisAPP represents the expected quantities. It is up to cadisAPP users to record the actual quantities.

Open the context menu at shipment level. You can click on « Shipment details » to read only the pallets details or « Loading equipment details » to edit the pallets types & quantities

With the scan of last package of a shipment, cadisAPP will ask from users to enter the actual quantities of pallets.

How to report pallet types & quantities

At any stage ((un)loading, delivery, inventory), the process is the same: you can record pallets quantities at shipment level as well as list level.

How to report a discrepancy on pallets

The actual number of pallets may be different from the reality. Also, the pallets may be in a bad shape. Here’s how to report a discrepancy on pallets.

❶ After you have scanned all packages & cadisAPP shows you the dialogue for recording actual numbers, click on the discrepancy icon. When you select a reason for the discrepancy, the pallet will be declared « Non Exchangeable » (NEX). Then, set the quantity NEX pallet & add a description (optional). Use the -/+ to edit the quantities of selected pallets or you can add a new pallet type.

❷ Your changes are visible in the overview of this pallet as well as in the pallet overview at trip level
