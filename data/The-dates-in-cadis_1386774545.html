CADIS Knowledge Base : The dates in cadis

Created by Mohamed.Shebl, last modified by Julian Beyer on Sep 08, 2025

The Collection & Delivery Dates

One for pick-up date of your shipments, the other for their delivery. These dates are taken into account for the trip optimization & will be shown on the back-office & drivers app. These dates can be sent from a host system or defined manually in cadis.

The Execution Date

The processing & display date of the shipments. CADIS will not auto-dispatch the shipments until the execution date has been reached. Also, after you have modified a rules engine criteria, you must update the concerned shipments execution date in cadis back-office for the new rule to apply
