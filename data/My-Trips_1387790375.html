CADIS Knowledge Base : My Trips

Created by Mohamed.Shebl, last modified by Julian Beyer on Sep 08, 2025

Drivers can optimize their trips stops sequence before leaving depot with MyTrips, a feature available directly on their mobile devices.

With MyTrips, Drivers can

  • Optimize the sequence of their stops

  • Change manually the sequence of stops

  • Check shipment’s details (clients, weight, map, requirements…)

  • Re-arrange their trips according to their needs & convenience

  • PODs display order on the Runsheet will be adjusted to the chosen stop sequence

  • Prepare themselves according to the trip special requirements

How to visualize your trips

❶ You can open MyTrips directly from the dedicated menu of cadisAPP or through a loading list

❷ Click on the list of options at trip level, then, « Show trip details »

❸ You get access to the list of stops. You can expand them to find the shipments included in the stops. ❹ Drivers can also choose to remove some shipments off their trips. ❺ An option allows you to collapse or expand them all.

❻ The tab Information gives you all details related to your trip (time windows, parties involved, special attributes…)

❼ You can also visualize your trips stops in a map(stops have previously been geocoded to be displayed on the map)

How to optimize your trips stops sequence

  • ❶ You can change manually your stops sequence or launch the sequence optimizer:

    • ❷ Toc change the sequence manually, select your stops (1 or more at the time) & use the arrows to move them up or down. Click « Apply » when done

    • ❸ To optimize automatically, you only have to click the option & wait for cadis calculation. Reminder: all the stops must have been geocoded for stops optimization to be enabled)

Explore the Tabs at shipments level

❶ You can open some additional tabs that apply at shipment level. Click on a shipment to do so

❷ The info tab, with all details related to your shipment

❸ The Contact data are dedicated to parties involved. ❹ Note that you can also find the History of the shipment or Refresh displayed data

❺ The package tab: You can get access to detailed history of your packages

❻ The Goods Items tab (details on dimensions & weight, number of pallets vs cartons…)

MyTrips in your desktop browser

All the described features are available in cadisSTUDIO, in your desktop browser
