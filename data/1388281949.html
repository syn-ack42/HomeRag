CADIS Knowledge Base : Why was my shipment not considered in the auto dispatching function, although I selected it before?

Created by Mohamed.Shebl, last modified by Julian Beyer on Sep 08, 2025

Possible reasons for why the shipment was not considered, are following:

  • The execution date is in the future.

  • The shipment is not dispatchable.

  • It is a primary distribution shipment.

  • The trip could not be determined by postcode routing or rules engine.

  • The (recurring) trip, that has been determined for the shipment, is not existing or it is in a state, in that dispatching is no longer possible.

  • A dispatching lock is set for the shipment.
