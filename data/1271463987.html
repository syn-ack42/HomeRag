CADIS Knowledge Base : How can I plan a collection with an obstacle again (follow-up order)?

Created by Julian Beyer, last modified on Sep 08, 2025

Open the shipment overview, select the pick-up order and select the context menu “Set PUD status”.

In the “Change status” screen, click the “Close” button, select the relevant collection hindrance and enter the desired new collection date:
