Your shipments data, as sent by your shippers or consignees, may be erroneous with names or addresses wrongly written. As a result, collections & deliveries belong to different stops. CADIS, based upon a set of criteria, can help you merge these stops.

The process for merging stops

❶ Your host system
Before even your shipments reach cadis, you may adjust the shipments data in your host system. Names & addresses would be sent to cadis aligned, thus producing a unique stop

❷ cadisSTUDIO
You can create a complete database of stops in the Master data. Shipments are merged by CADIS at import stage (see section 2.1). Or, On a daily basis, dispatchers geocode shipments to merge them. CADIS keeps in memory the geoposition of the consignee/shipper for later imports.

❸ cadisAPP
While on tour, drivers may find out some shipments belong to the same stop. They have the possibility to merge (or unmerge) them in cadisAPP.
⚠️ CADIS does not keep in memory the actions of the driver for later imports.

Which criteria for matching?

❶ The StopLocationID
Your stop locations master data must be created upstream. Then the StopLocationID must be in sent in daily imports from your host system to cadis (see next section). Thus, all shipments bearing the same StopLocationID will be merged to the stop location defined at master data level.

❷ The MatchCode1
Both the Geo-coordinates, (tolerance zone is configurable, by default approx. 3m, or 100m per Lat/Long) & the Full Normalized name of consignee (CADIS filters for prefixes like “Mr”, “Mrs”, “Company”, “Dr”, or suffixes: “Co”,  etc)

❸ The MatchCode2
The string of the Normalized name & Address (zip code & street of the consignee – only the first 5 characters each criteria are considered)

The field “Name2” of your consignee or shipper can be used to separate delivery stops.

The StopLocationID

Create or Edit your stops locations

First, in the master data menu of cadisSTUDIO (❶), open the Stop Locations (❷), click on Create (❸) or double click on an existing stop to edit it.

Enter a StopID (❹) as a free text, that will be used fro matching with your shipments. The customer’s data (name & address) are mandatory fields.

(❺) CADIS automatically geolocates your stop. If not, it remains possible to geolocate manually at shipment level, in cadisCONTROL.

The stops locations properties

You can define the properties of your stop in Delivery & Collection tabs.

❶ The “Collections properties” & “Delivery properties” share the same layout

❷ Person in charge at the stop location for deliveries or collections

❸ The time necessary for the driver to deliver or collect goods are considered for the sequence optimization

❹ Special attributes vehicle will need to deliver or collect goods

❺ The Instructions are addressed to drivers or terminal workers. For more info, click here

❻ Choose the opening hours for delivery or collection at this stop (more detailed behaviour of cadis below). Also, the “closed” tick is reported in the schedule for collection template.
