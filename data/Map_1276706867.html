Open the Map screen using the :Karte: symbol in the cadis control panel.

In the map you can display vehicles and their orders.

Toolbar

:refreshRed:Refreshes the view

:kopieren:: Copies the map view to the clipboard

:auswählen:: Selects the map section so that all filtered vehicles and orders are displayed.

The Vehicles filter area

Selects which vehicles should be displayed.
The individual filters are linked with AND. You may have to reset a filter to All if you want to use another filter.

The Orders filter area

Selects which orders of the filtered vehicles should be displayed.

The General area

Selects whether the distance travelled and / or the remaining distance for the vehicles should be displayed, whether details of vehicles and orders should be displayed.

The traffic information view shows traffic jams, road works, road closures etc.
