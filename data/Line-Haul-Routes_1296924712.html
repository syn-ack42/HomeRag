CADIS Knowledge Base : Line Haul Routes

Created by Julian Beyer, last modified on Aug 21, 2025

You can reach the screen for creating Line haul routes by using the master data overview (:regeldefinition: symbol in the cadis control panel)

Line haul trips are always created for a specific line haul route from the master data.

For a line haul trip, an outbound route is represented by the area of the destination depot and the driving area of a transport unit.

Line haul routes are usually operated according to a fixed timetable within the framework of a line haul trip.

The fields marked with an asterisk are mandatory.
