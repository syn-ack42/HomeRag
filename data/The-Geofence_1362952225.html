CADIS Knowledge Base : The Geofence

Created by Julian Beyer, last modified on Sep 08, 2025
  • 1 Prerequisite: License & ConfigManager
  • 2 Reading the data
  • 3 Exporting the data

cadis back office reports the time stamps of the driver entering & exiting the area surrounding the stop location. This area is called the Geofence. The data is also exported to your host system.

Prerequisite: License & ConfigManager

Exporting the data

The events exported from cadis to your host system are:

Status

Sub-status

Definition

2600

0

Stop area reached (the driver has entered the Geofence area)

2601

0

Leave stop area (the driver has left the Geofence area)

2650

0

Stop in approach (the driver is heading for the next stop*)

* This event will only be triggered if the driver respects the sequence of the trip because cadis must be aware of the next stop to be processed (i.e. if the driver processes “StopC” before “StopB”, the event will not be triggered for “StopB”). Also, note that this event will only be triggered for export after the stop is closed. This is to make sure the driver did not enter the geofence area just because he was passing by.

NOTE: cadis also exports some events similar to the ones above, but they are not related to the Geofencing feature. Those events are:
