CADIS Knowledge Base : The Handover

Created by Mohamed.Shebl, last modified by Julian Beyer on Sep 08, 2025

While On tour, the feature "Handover" allows two drivers to meet so that driver A can pass on one or more delivery orders to driver B.

Configuration

An action in the ConfigManager of cadisSTUDIO is necessary to enable the feature
