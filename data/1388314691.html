CADIS Knowledge Base : My mobile device is not scanning – why?

Created by Mohamed.Shebl, last modified by Julian Beyer on Sep 08, 2025

For some devices it is not that easy possible to trigger scanning because of their design. In this case you can fade in the scan button via the settings.

With this “screen button” you can trigger the scan.
