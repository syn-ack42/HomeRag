CADIS Knowledge Base : How to use the cadisSTUDIO Arrival board

Created by Mohamed.Shebl, last modified by Julian Beyer on Sep 08, 2025

In cadisSTUDIO you can follow along the planned unloadings und their status in the arrival board. All trips, which are expected in the depot to be unloaded there are listed in a table.

Following information is visible among others:

  • Name of the trip

  • Freight carrier

  • Planned arrival time

  • Unloading status and progress

  • Assigned loading units

By default they are sorted by their planned arrival time (ascending).

Only one trip can be selected at a time. After selection, details about the trip are displayed on the right:

  • products on the trip

  • priority shipments on the trip

and how many shipments are affected.
