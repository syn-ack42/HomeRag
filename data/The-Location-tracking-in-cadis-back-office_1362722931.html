ETA vs PTA

  • PTA (Planned Arrival Time) = calculated by the server after sequence optimization which considers the time windows.

  • ETA (Estimated Time Arrival) = transmitted by the mobile device every 5min if connection is OK. If not, you‚Äôll see an icon ‚Äúrefresh‚Äù:

This means that the ETA will always be more accurate (closer to reality) than the PTA. You can observe this by comparing columns ‚ÄúETA‚Äù vs ‚Äúbegin/end of Stop‚Äù

You may find the difference between ETA vs PTA increases with time. This is a sign your optimization settings can be improved to stick better to reality. You can try adjusting the handling time at stop level. In cadis, it is configured by default to 5 min).

Another setting that could be changed would be the vehicle speed. A task performed by cadis team, & which would affect all your agencies . It therefore requires coordination.
