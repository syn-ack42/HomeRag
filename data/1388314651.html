CADIS Knowledge Base : How can I plan another delivery for a shipment with delivery hindrance?

Created by Mohamed.Shebl, last modified by Julian Beyer on Sep 08, 2025

If for a shipment or a partial shipment (dispatching status says "not dispatchable" a hindrance was set, it is possible to change the dispatching status into "to be dispatched" by click on the corresponding context menu entry.

After doing that, the shipment can be planned for another trip again.
