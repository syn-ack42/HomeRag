CADIS Knowledge Base : How to use the cadisSTUDIO Dashboard

Created by Mohamed.Shebl, last modified by Julian Beyer on Sep 08, 2025

General information

In cadisSTUDIO a quick overview of the current status of the loadings can be viewed in a dashboard using metrics.

  • Loading status

  • Departure times

  • Outbound status of the dispatched shipments

  • Inbound status of the dispatched shipments

  • Inbound status of the shipment which are still to be planned

  • Packages with deviations in the loading

Required licences

  • License for terminal handling

  • License for area freight forwarder (if it relevant to be filtered)

  • Temperatures are only displayed in these metrics if a temperature license is available.

Overall view

Filter and grouping

Departure metrics are displayed on a daily basis, grouped by departure time, outbound route and are freight forwarder.

You can also choose whether the metrics are displayed for last mile or line haul or for all departures.

All departures that are not otherwise grouped can be found under “-NA-“ (for example, because no planned departure time is given).

Metrics

Loading status

These statuses can be clicked on in the graphic (circle). You then switch to “My loadings”, which is filtered according to the selected status. By clicking on a status text, it can be hidden in the graphic.

Departure times – traffic light

The warning levels of the departure times can be clicked on in the graphic. You then switch to “My loadings”, which is filtered according to the chosen criteria.

Red: departure time is passed

Yellow: departure time within the next 15 minutes

Green. departure time in 15 minutes at the earliest
